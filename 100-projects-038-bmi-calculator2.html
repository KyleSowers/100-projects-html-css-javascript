BMI Calculator

BMI, or Body Mass Index, is a measure used to determine whether a person has a healthy body weight for a given height. It is defined as the individual's body mass divided by the square of their height. The unit of BMI is kg/mÂ².

The BMI cartegories for adults according to the World Health Organization (WHO) are:
- Below 18.5: Underweight
- 18.5-24.9: Normal Weight
- 25-29.9: Overweight
- 30 and above: Obesity

Please note that while BMI is a useful screening tool, it does not directly measure body fat. It's always a good idea to consult with a healthcare professional for a full understanding of your health.
